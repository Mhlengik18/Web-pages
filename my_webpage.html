Historic places in Johannesburg

Ponte City

Ponte city ,aka Vodacom tower, was built in 1975. It is a 55 storey cylindrical building with a height of 173m, making it the tallest residential skyscraper in Africa. The neon sign on top of the building is the largest sign in the southern hemisphere.

MTN Taxi rank

MTN Taxi rank is one of the two largest taxi ranks in the country. It shelters taxis that transport thousands of commuters every day that travel to and from all areas in Gauteng. It is located in central Johannesburg.

Victoria Yards

Victoria Yards is a creative hub that is home to an expansive farm and a community of artists, artisans and makers whose talents include jewellery design, fashion, baking, painting, furniture making, etc. The buildings are restored industrial heritage buildings located in Lorentzville. On open days resident artists open they're studios to the public.
